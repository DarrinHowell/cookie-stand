It started with one cookie cutter. With the PNW being our stomping grounds, our family had made it a tradition to bake cookies that reminded us of the natural beauty of the Northwest.

In addition to packing our cookies with loce we decided to throw some secret ingredients from our family recipe into our salmon cookies.

Before long, word got out, and our every neighbor on our street began clamoring for our secret recipe. My wife and I both had an "Aha" moment. We both had enterpeneural inclincations, and one night, we sat down and started drafting the blueprints of what would be the start of us living and seizing our dreams.

That was ten years ago, and after starting our first cookie stand at Pike's Place Market, we've launched 4 additional stores and have plans for 5 new sites.
